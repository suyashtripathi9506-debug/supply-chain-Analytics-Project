# supply-chain-Analytics-Project
1. Product Distribution

Description:
This chart shows the distribution of orders across different products. It helps identify the most frequently ordered products and understand product demand patterns.

2. Category Distribution

Description:
This visualization represents the number of orders in each product category. It helps analyze which categories contribute the most to overall sales.

3. Supplier Distribution

Description:
This chart displays the number of orders handled by each supplier. It is useful for evaluating supplier contribution and dependency.

4. Warehouse Distribution

Description:
This graph shows how orders are distributed across warehouses. It helps identify warehouse workload and storage utilization.

5. Quantity Distribution

Description:
This histogram illustrates the distribution of order quantities. It helps understand ordering behavior and inventory requirements.

6. Unit Price Distribution

Description:
This chart shows the spread of product unit prices. It helps identify pricing trends and product value ranges.

7. Inventory Level Distribution

Description:
This visualization represents inventory levels across products. It helps monitor stock availability and inventory management efficiency.

8. Delivery Days Distribution

Description:
This graph shows the distribution of delivery times. It helps evaluate logistics performance and delivery efficiency.

9. Supplier vs Product

Description:
This chart compares suppliers across different products. It helps identify which suppliers provide specific products and their order volumes.

10. Product vs Category

Description:
This visualization shows the relationship between products and categories. It helps understand product classification and category coverage.

Project Description (Resume/Portfolio)

Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard:
Analyzed a supply chain dataset containing 500 orders to evaluate product demand, supplier performance, inventory levels, warehouse utilization, and delivery efficiency. Created visualizations to identify trends, optimize inventory management, and support data-driven supply chain decisions using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Power BI.
